Perfume Chemist

A Perfume Chemist, also known as a Fragrance Chemist or Perfumer, is a specialist who formulates and develops scents by blending natural and synthetic ingredients. They work with essential oils, aroma compounds, and fixatives to create perfumes.

Essential Oil

Essential oils are highly concentrated, aromatic liquids extracted from plants through distillation (steam/water) or cold pressing. They capture the plant's natural scent, flavor, and therapeutic properties.
